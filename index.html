Creator workflow tool

JarretPosts helps authorized creators prepare TikTok draft uploads.

JarretPosts is an early development tool for users who authorize their own TikTok account and upload their own original videos as TikTok drafts for final review and posting in TikTok.

What The App Does

The app connects to TikTok only after a user completes TikTok OAuth authorization. It uses that authorization to upload creator-selected video content as a draft to the connected TikTok account.

JarretPosts is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or sponsored by TikTok. TikTok users remain responsible for the content they choose to upload and for complying with TikTok's terms, community guidelines, and music usage rules.
