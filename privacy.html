Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 30, 2026

Overview

JarretPosts is a creator workflow tool that lets authorized users upload their own original videos as TikTok drafts. This policy explains what information the app uses and how it is handled.

Information We Access

When you connect TikTok, TikTok may provide account authorization information such as your open ID, basic profile information, access token, refresh token, token expiration time, and granted scopes. The app uses this information only to authenticate your account and perform actions you authorize.

Content You Provide

If you choose to upload a video, the app processes the video file and related draft information only for the purpose of sending it to TikTok as a draft upload.

How Information Is Used

We use TikTok authorization data to connect your account, refresh access when needed, identify the connected account, and upload content you explicitly choose to send to TikTok.

Sharing

We do not sell personal information. Information is shared with TikTok only as needed to provide the TikTok upload functionality you request.

Retention And Deletion

OAuth tokens and related account connection data are kept only as long as needed to provide the connected account workflow. You may request disconnection or deletion by contacting us.

Security

We use reasonable safeguards to protect account connection data. No internet service can guarantee perfect security, so users should disconnect access if they no longer use the app.

Contact

For privacy questions or deletion requests, contact jarret@example.com.
